# LIQWD Product Snapshot — 2026-07-14

A periodic state-of-the-product pitch for our two audiences. Regenerate this
when things change; hand it back to Claude to re-establish context.

Positioning: **LIQWD is the platform for NEW & pre-construction homes** — a
consumer marketplace, a verified-agent portal, and a developer distribution
channel. We **never take a commission**: revenue is flat fees, subscriptions,
and credits.

---

## FOR REALTORS

**Elevator:** A free, verified home for new-construction agents. Every pre-con
project in one place, a public profile that wins you leads, and tools that turn
projects into your pipeline — no referral fees, no brokerage change, you keep
100%.

### Built today
- **Free verified public profile / link-in-bio** (`/realtors/{you}` + `/@you`):
  photo, banner (auto-pulled from project imagery or your own), bio, service
  area, custom links, and a per-agent social card that unfurls with your face
  in iMessage / WhatsApp / Instagram DMs.
- **Verified badge** — a live, revocable "RECO Verified · LIQWD" image for your
  email signature, website, and socials (regulator-accurate, TRESA-safe).
- **Achievement medals** computed from real activity (Founding Agent, Project
  Steward, Project Scout, Network Builder) + self-reported awards.
- **Attributed share links** — every project has your personal link; buyer
  inquiries route to you, attributed, no fee. Free for all verified agents,
  and attribution now survives the buyer clicking around (30-day cookie).
- **Shortlists** — curate a handful of projects for a specific client, send one
  link; every inquiry on it is yours, even when the client forwards it around.
- **Buyer portal** (on Shortlists) — attach floor plans, incentives, and
  deposit terms for your client. Gated: sent/shared only, never on the public
  site or in search.
- **Free leads** — claim or add a project and its buyer inquiries route to you.
- **Leads inbox** (`/dashboard/leads`) — every inquiry routed to you in one
  place, with contact details and a new → contacted → qualified → won/lost
  pipeline; instant email alert deep-links to it. Empty state frames the
  free-leads promise honestly ("inbox is live, volume ramping" — capture
  always on, no benchmarks promised).
- **Lead Pages + analytics** — see link views (30 days) and attributed leads;
  a weekly digest emails you what your links did.
- **Off-market board** — agent-to-agent listings, gated to verified realtors.
- **One-click builder portals** — every developer's broker portal in one place.
- **Instant RECO verification** (+ BC / Florida), buyer matching, proposals /
  negotiate terms, quick wins, refer & earn, submit-a-project, update requests.
- **Activation tracker + celebration moments** (verified, first lead).

### Noted for the future
- **Assignment desk** — gated pre-construction assignment board (spec'd).
- **Allocations marketplace** — compete for developer allocations, flat fee
  (spec'd, pending legal).
- **Auto-generated co-branded PDF fact sheets** for any project.
- **Email-signature widget** showing your live tracked projects.
- **"Verified pre-con realtors in {city}" directory pages** (once agent density
  supports it).
- **Top Contributor tiers** + priority routing of house (unattributed) leads.
- **Register-to-unlock** automation for the buyer portal.
- **Per-file curation** of which shared materials appear on your shortlists.

## FOR DEVELOPERS

**Elevator:** Put your project in front of the verified agents and ready buyers
who actually sell new construction — featured placement across agent pages and
the consumer marketplace, targeted agent eBlasts, and live demand data. Flat /
credit pricing; we never take a cut of your commission.

### Built today
- **Featured & advertiser placements** — your project is force-fed into
  verified agents' free public pages by geography, plus the consumer
  marketplace grid.
- **eBlasts** to verified agents.
- **Deal desk / move inventory** — surface inventory to ready agents.
- **Ready buyers** — buyer mandates matched to your project.
- **Rental referrals** lane.
- **Developer connections** (credit-based intros).
- **Demand & analytics research** view.
- **Zero-effort ingestion** — forward a marketing email and it becomes a live
  project page; the discovery engine also finds your projects from permits,
  aggregators, and portals.

### Noted for the future
- **Allocations marketplace** — post allocations, verified agents compete for
  them; flat access fees, never a commission cut (spec'd, pending legal).
- **Deeper demand dashboards** (which markets/agents are pulling your project).
- **Secondary-market intel** via the assignment desk.

## THE ENGINE UNDERNEATH (serves both)

- **Consumer marketplace + programmatic SEO** — ~1,150 projects with
  individual pages, city hubs (`/new-homes/{city}`), builder hubs, buyer tools
  (land-transfer, deposit, and HST-rebate calculators), full schema + IndexNow.
  Markets: **Ontario, BC, Alberta, Florida (226 live), Texas, Tennessee,
  California.**
- **Discovery + auto-dressing** — permit feeds, aggregators, and builder
  portals feed a pipeline that AI-sources hero images and generates SEO copy on
  publish.
- **Recruitment machine** — compliant cold-email waves to a 10k+ verified-agent
  list (two waves live: inventory pitch + "your page is live" claim pitch).
